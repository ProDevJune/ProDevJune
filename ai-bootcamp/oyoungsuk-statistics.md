### 2025.04.04 (금) — Descriptive Statistics for Artificial Intelligence

- **목표:** 기초 통계 개념부터 확률 분포까지 실습 기반 학습

- **교시별 구성:**
  1. Statistics의 개요
  2. Number of cases
  3. Probability Theory
  4. Variables & Scales
  5. Population & Sample
  6. Descriptive Statistics
  7. Random Variable
  8. Probability distribution
